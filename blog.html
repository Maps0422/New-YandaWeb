Allan Gray Makers Competition

On the 20th of July, Yanda Centre of Innovation and Entrepreneurship was invited by Nkangala TVET College where we were asked to be assist student entrepreneurs for an Allan Gray Makers Pitch Competition. Our role was to assist student entrepreneurs to identify a problem within their business sector, have a solution for it, bring about a market traction, have a revenue model as well as competitive analysis. Because of the vast experience that our Centre Manage with local and international pitching, Lucky Netshidzati assisted the student entrepreneurs with valuable pointers that a funder would mainly look at and consider funding possibilities.

First Yanda Center

Our Centre, located at Nkangala TVET College's Middelburg Campus in the heart of Mpumalanga's farming and industrial region, serves as a hub of innovation for young entrepreneurs in the Enkangala District. Here, we transform the ideas of our young innovators into market-ready products, fostering a culture of creativity and entrepreneurship.

Cybersecurity Training

At Yanda, we are committed to supporting our SMEs in navigating the digital world. In an era of increasing reliance on digital innovations, robust security is essential. That's why we offer tailored Cybersecurity training to equip our SMEs with the knowledge they need to operate safely online, ensuring the highest level of protection in the digital space.
