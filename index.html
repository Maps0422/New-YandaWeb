    About Us

    Yanda Centre of Innovation & Entrepreneurship is dedicated to empowering entrepreneurs by offering comprehensive support across various critical areas. The centre has successfully supported numerous SMEs, including those owned by blind and deaf entrepreneurs, fostering inclusivity and innovation. In addition to assisting entrepreneurs, the centre provides skill development training in the science, technology, and engineering sectors, specifically tailored to empower blind and deaf unemployed youth. Through its placement program, the centre connects these skilled individuals with SMEs it supports, enabling meaningful employment opportunities and strengthening the entrepreneurial ecosystem.

    Key focus areas of the centre include ideation creation, business development strategies, innovative business models, design thinking methodologies, access to funding platforms, intellectual property (IP) registration guidance, and prototype design and printing services. The centre supports both abled and disabled individuals, ensuring an inclusive approach to innovation and entrepreneurship.
