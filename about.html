About Us

Yanda Centre of Innovation & Entrepreneurship aims to empower entrepreneurs in the Mpumalanga region by providing comprehensive assistance across various crucial areas.

Crucial areas include ideation creation, business development strategies, innovative business models, design thinking methodologies, access to funding platforms, intellectual property (IP) registration guidance, as well as prototype design and printing services

Our vision is to be the catalyst for transformative growth in the CIE sector, empowering entrepreneurs to innovate, create sustainable businesses, and drive economic development.

We strive to create a vibrant ecosystem where creativity thrives, ideas flourish, and businesses succeed.
